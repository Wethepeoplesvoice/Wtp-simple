America's Watchlist

The People's Eyes on Power.

Imagine one place where Americans can finally see what government is doing: bills, votes, taxes, power hikes, and influence — without having to dig through dozens of sites or legal pages. America’s Watchlist is a peaceful, citizen-powered platform to keep an eye on power so you don’t have to.

Example Alerts

These are sample alerts to show how America’s Watchlist could surface what’s really happening — fast, simple, and in plain language.

Red Flag · Spending
Midnight spending bill with hidden raises & new fees

A 900-page spending bill is rushed through with only hours to read it. Buried inside: automatic pay raises for officials and new fees on utility bills. America’s Watchlist flags this as a Red Flag alert so citizens can see who voted for it.

National · Prototype
Power & Utilities
State-wide power rate increase tied to “infrastructure fee”

Your state approves a 14% increase to power bills, calling it an “infrastructure fee.” America’s Watchlist would show when it passed, who voted yes, and how much extra it will cost the average household each year.

State Demo · Georgia
Property & Taxes
Quiet change to property tax formula

A small line in a “technical corrections” bill changes the way property values are calculated, pushing taxes higher over time. America’s Watchlist flags this as a Watch alert so homeowners can see the long-term impact.

State Demo · South Carolina

State & National Watch

In a full build, America’s Watchlist would give each state its own dashboard — so citizens can see alerts that matter where they live, along with a national view of what’s happening across all 50 states.

State Watch · Example
Georgia · Bills, Power & Surpluses

A Georgia dashboard could show current bills, power-company hikes, budget surpluses, and how those surpluses are used: refunded, saved, or spent. The goal is simple: no more guessing what happened at the Capitol.

Join America's Watchlist (Demo)

This is where the movement becomes real. Use this demo form as your first live “join” page to collect supporters by state. Later, a developer can hook it into a real database or email system.

When you join America’s Watchlist, you’re raising your hand to say: “The people are watching now.” In a full version, this form would add you to your state’s list so you could receive alerts, see dashboards, and join future town halls.

  • No parties. No violence. No special interests.
  • Just citizens who want to see what government is doing.
  • One list, one movement, one nation watching together.

Demo notice: this form is wired to Formspree so you can start collecting real sign-ups without building a full backend yet.

After you hit submit, Formspree will send your entry to the email connected to this form. Later, a developer can replace this with a custom database and login system.
