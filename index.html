Example Alerts & Signals
What America’s Watchlist Could Surface
When America’s Watchlist is fully built, alerts would light up whenever something important happens — not years later, but while citizens can still act. Here are example alert types:
Red Flag · Big Money Influence
Massive Donation Before a Key Vote
A senator receives a huge donation days before voting on a bill. America’s Watchlist would flag this as a red flag so citizens can see who paid, who voted, and what it might cost them.
Spending & Surplus
State Surplus with No Relief
Your state ends the year with a giant surplus while families struggle with power bills and groceries. An alert shows the surplus, where the money went, and gives citizens a way to demand relief.
Power & Utilities
Sudden Power Bill Hikes
Utility companies raise rates again. America’s Watchlist would show the hike, the vote that allowed it, and which leaders approved it — in one simple dashboard.
Hidden Bills
Sneaky Riders & Fine Print
Long bills with buried riders are hard to track. The platform could highlight hidden provisions, rushed votes, and last-minute amendments so citizens see the truth before it passes.
National & State Town Halls
AI Voiceover · Visual Streams · No Camera Required
America’s Watchlist could host regular national briefings and state-level town halls whenever alerts cross an important threshold — so people don’t just see the data, they hear it explained in plain language.
