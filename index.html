Why This Matters

A Peaceful Movement for Accountability

Government has grown bigger, more complicated, and harder to follow. Most people are working, raising families, and trying to survive — they don’t have hours to dig through bills, budgets, and legal language.

That gap is where bad decisions, quiet deals, and unfair policies slip through. Billionaires, lobbyists, and giant corporations have learned how to use that gap to pull the strings — while the people who are supposed to be in charge are left in the dark.

Many elected officials have forgotten who they work for. They stay in power for decades, grow wealthy on influence, and listen more to donors and TV cameras than the people back home. As long as there are no real term limits and no real transparency, trust in government will keep collapsing.

America’s Watchlist exists to peacefully flip that script: to put power back in the hands of informed citizens, to expose what’s really happening in plain language, and to give millions of people a simple way to act together — before it’s too late to fix.

National Petition – America’s Watchlist

Bring Back Accountability & Term Limits

Citizens can sign this petition and then send the same message directly to Congress using official contact pages. In a full build, verified U.S.-citizen logins and secure vote counts would power national mandates.

To the elected officials of the United States:

We, the undersigned citizens, are deeply concerned about the growing distance between government power and the people it is supposed to serve.

We call for:

1. Real-time transparency for major federal spending, rate hikes, and new taxes that impact households.

2. Clear public disclosure of large donations, gifts, and foreign-linked income for all members of Congress.

3. Stronger and faster oversight when officials abuse their office, ignore the will of the people, or undermine trust in our institutions.

4. Reasonable term limits for members of Congress, so no one can turn public service into a permanent, untouchable career. Power was never meant to be held for decades by the same people, shielded by donors and party machines.

We believe these reforms are non-partisan. They are not about left or right — they are about restoring trust, accountability, and a government that remembers who it works for: the American people.

We ask you to support meaningful reforms that bring power back to the people and rebuild the trust that has been lost.
