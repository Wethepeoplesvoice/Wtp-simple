The alert system – heartbeat of the movement

Alerts are how the people stay ahead of corruption. Below is a live-style preview of national alerts United by Truth could push out right now, followed by the six core alert types your platform will launch with.

Red Flag · Sneaky · Spending · Power · Votes · Crisis
Live National Alerts (Sample) What members across all 50 states would see
Red Flag Bills
Harmful, corrupt, or dangerous bills that threaten rights, explode spending, or give government more power over people without clear consent.
Example: midnight vote to expand surveillance or new tax hidden in a “safety bill”.
Sneaky Bills
Shady provisions hidden inside giant bills: where they try to bury giveaways, immunity, or rule changes under “emergency” or “infrastructure” language.
Example: political pay raise hidden inside disaster funding.
Spending Alerts
Where the money is really going: billion-dollar contracts, pork projects, backroom deals, and “emergency” funds that never reach the people.
Example: state surplus used on pet projects instead of returning money to citizens.
Power & Utility Alerts
Power bill hikes, monopoly utility deals, and corruption around basic services that families rely on every month.
Example: double-digit power hike while executives get bonuses.
Politician Voting Alerts
How your representatives actually voted — not the spin. Every major vote tied to their name, donors, and promises.
Example: “Your senator voted YES on Bill 412 backed by donors X, Y, Z.”
State Crisis Alerts
Urgent state events: rushed bills, emergency powers, scandals, resignations, or major protests that require immediate attention.
Example: “State attempts to fast-track new law without hearings tomorrow at 9AM.”
