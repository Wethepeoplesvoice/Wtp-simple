For years we’ve watched the same people stay in power, the same donors pull the strings, and the same media cycles keep us angry at each other instead of focused on the truth.

America’s Torch is about millions of ordinary citizens standing together — peacefully — to watch power, expose corruption, demand transparency, and make it impossible to pretend we’re not paying attention.

The goal is simple: one place where citizens can see what’s happening, vote on what they want to fight, sign petitions, send complaints, and support a movement that refuses to let the flame of accountability go out.

WHY NOW If we don’t stand together and apply peaceful pressure now, the people who benefit from confusion and division will keep using it forever. America’s Torch exists so we can say, “We see you. We’re done being ignored.”

What This Platform Is Built To Do

See What’s Really Happening

Future dashboards will show bills, votes, rate hikes, and spending in simple language — so citizens can spot problems before it’s too late.

Vote & Sign Petitions

Citizens will be able to vote once per issue and sign focused petitions that go straight to leaders instead of disappearing into social media.

File Real Complaints

Instead of hunting for forms, citizens will be guided to real ethics, DOJ, and state complaint routes when officials cross the line.

Stay 100% Peaceful

No riots, no threats, no violence. Just citizens watching together, signing together, and speaking together in numbers too big to wave away.

PETITION #1

Real Transparency and Accountability

To the elected officials of the United States:

We, the undersigned citizens, call for real-time transparency for major spending, clear disclosure of big donors and conflicts, and faster action when officials abuse their office or ignore the will of the people.

  1. Public, simple tools to track major federal and state spending.
  2. Full disclosure of large donations and financial conflicts.
  3. Stronger, faster oversight when the public trust is broken.
Demo only: signatures are emailed to a secure inbox via Formspree. Real signature counts and deliveries will require a backend later.
