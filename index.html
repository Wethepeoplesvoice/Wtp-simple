National Petition – America’s Watchlist

Citizens can sign this petition and then send the same message directly to Congress using official contact pages.

Petition Text

To the elected officials of the United States: We, the undersigned citizens, are deeply concerned about the growing distance between government power and the people it is supposed to serve. We call for: 1. Real-time transparency for major federal spending, rate hikes, and new taxes that impact households. 2. Clear, public disclosure of large donations, gifts, and foreign-linked income for all members of Congress. 3. Stronger and faster oversight when officials appear to abuse their office, ignore the will of the people, or undermine trust in our institutions. We believe these reforms are non-partisan. They are not about left or right — they are about honesty, accountability, and restoring trust. We respectfully urge you to: – Support stronger disclosure and ethics rules, – Defend the rights of citizens to know how power is being used, – And remember that your authority comes from us, the people. Sincerely, Citizens of the United States, signing through America’s Watchlist.

Complaints & Ethics Reports

When something crosses the line, citizens shouldn’t have to dig for forms and email addresses. These tools draft a complaint and open your email app with everything filled in.

Federal Complaint Menu

Choose a federal target, describe the issue, and click Open Email Draft. Your email program will open with the correct address, subject, and body ready to send.

DOJ’s public email AskDOJ@usdoj.gov is routed inside the Department. You can edit everything before you hit send.

State Forms & Complaints

Use this for state-level issues (governor, legislature, state AG, or ethics offices). You or a developer can set a permanent address for each state.

Once you’ve set correct emails for each state office, people in that state can send complaints in a few clicks, from their own inbox.
