top issues we’re watching right now

1) Spending Without Clear Limits

Large funding bills and programs with vague standards and weak oversight.

Red flag: accountability and transparency.

2) Delegation to Agencies

Congress writes broad language and agencies write the real rules.

Red flag: blurred responsibility.

3) Property & Due Process

Enforcement practices that punish beyond debt or deny meaningful review.

Red flag: rights without remedies.

4) Court Access & Remedies

Policies that make it harder to challenge unconstitutional action.

Red flag: procedural barriers.

5) Conflicts of Interest

Voting and spending tied to donors, contractors, and revolving doors.

Red flag: trust breakdown.
